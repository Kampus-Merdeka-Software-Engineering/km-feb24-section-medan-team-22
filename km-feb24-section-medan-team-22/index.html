Brand Story

Kenalin, kami....



Pizza Shop was founded in 2003 with a passion for authentic Italian cuisine, particularly pizza. he company started as a small shop in the heart of Naples, Italy before expanding to the USA, where they opened their first branch in New York


Since its inception, Pizza Shop has experienced rapid growth. From a small shop in Naples, we now have 25 of branches across Europe, North America, and Asia. Our expansion continues to bring authentic Italian flavors to more people around the world.


Pizza Shop vision is to serve unbeatable authentic Italian pizza to customers worldwide. Their mission is to use high-quality ingredients, traditional manufacturing processes, and friendly service to provide an unforgettable culinary experience.


We are committed to using the finest available ingredients, from organic tomato sauce to high-quality mozzarella cheese, and pizza dough made fresh every day using time-honored recipes. We believe that quality should never be compromised, and we always strive to deliver consistent taste in every slice of pizza we serve .Over the years, Pizza Shop has received several awards and accolades for our dedication to quality and innovation in the pizza industry.
